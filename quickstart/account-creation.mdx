1. First of all, [create a new Edgee account](https://www.edgee.cloud/login).
2. Please, be aware that creating an account means that you agree to our [Terms of Service](https://www.edgee.cloud/terms) and [Privacy Policy](https://www.edgee.cloud/privacy).
3. You will receive an email with a link to validate your email address.

You are now ready to [create your first API key](/quickstart/api-key)!
