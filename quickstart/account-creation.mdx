title: Account Creation
description: Create a new Edgee account.
icon: chevrons-right
---

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign up">
    Go to [edgee.cloud/login](https://www.edgee.cloud/login) and create your account using your email.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify your email">
    Check your inbox and click the verification link.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Access the console">
    Once verified, you'll land on your Edgee Console dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create your organization">
    From your dashboard, create a new organization to manage your models and API keys.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  By creating an account, you agree to our [Terms of Service](https://www.edgee.cloud/terms) and [Privacy Policy](https://www.edgee.cloud/privacy).
</Info>
