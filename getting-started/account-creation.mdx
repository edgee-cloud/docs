1. First of all, [create a new Edgee account](https://www.edgee.cloud/register).
2. Fill in your details, including your name, email, and password.
3. Please, be aware that creating an account means that you agree to our [Terms of Service](https://www.edgee.cloud/terms) and [Privacy Policy](https://www.edgee.cloud/privacy).
4. You will receive an email with a link to validate your email address.

You are now ready to [create your first project](/getting-started/project-settings)!
